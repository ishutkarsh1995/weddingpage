Places to Visit Around Raipur

A modern riverfront-style walkway around Telibandha Lake, famous for its vibrant night lighting, food stalls, cycling tracks, and the iconic Peace Statue. One of Raipur‚Äôs most lively hangout spots.

Buddha Talab (Vivekananda Sarovar)

Located at the heart of Raipur, this lake features a massive Vivekananda statue, boating, illuminated pathways, and peaceful gardens. It is a perfect spot for evening walks and photography.

Mayfair Lake Resort, Raipur

One of Raipur‚Äôs most luxurious properties, Mayfair Lake Resort, is located in Naya Raipur (approx. 20 km from Raipur city) beside a scenic lake. Bhilai Steel Plant

Located about 20 km from the marriage palace, the Bhilai Steel Plant stands as a testament to India‚Äôs industrial excellence. Visitors can witness advanced steel-making processes, explore a well-designed township, and enjoy the green surroundings that beautifully blend nature with engineering marvels. Visiting the place might require a pre-approval/special permission.

Nandanvan Jungle Safari

One of India's largest man-made safari parks, located in Naya Raipur. Visitors can explore the tiger safari, lion safari, herbivore zones, aviary, and reptile houses. A perfect family outing spot just 30 km from Raipur city.

Dongargarh

A major pilgrimage site located 65 km from Raipur, Dongargarh is renowned for the Maa Bamleshwari Temple, situated atop a 1,600-foot hill. A ropeway adds thrill to the experience. The vibrant Navratri fairs here attract thousands of devotees every year. The view from the top is majestic. It also offers an optional 1 hour trek option from the base of the hill to the temple at the top.

Gangrel Dam

Also known as the Ravishankar Dam, this scenic water reservoir is perfect for peaceful retreats and water activities. Located approximately 70 km from Raipur, it offers serene islands, boating options, and breathtaking views during the monsoon season.

Ghatarani Waterfall

A beautiful forest-surrounded waterfall located around 75 km from Raipur. Ghatarani is especially popular during monsoons for trekking, nature walks, and photography. The serene temple near the falls adds to its charm. One of the most visited natural spot near raipur.
